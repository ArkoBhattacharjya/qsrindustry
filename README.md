# qsrindustry
🍔 QSR Data Analysis Project
Optimizing Operational Efficiency using Data-Driven Insights
📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) industry using structured data and SQL-based analytics. It demonstrates how data can be leveraged to improve operational efficiency, customer experience, and business performance.

The project combines business understanding + database design + data analysis to derive actionable insights.

🚀 Objectives
Analyze sales trends and revenue patterns
Identify top and low-performing menu items
Evaluate delivery efficiency and delays
Understand customer behavior and retention
Optimize inventory and store performance
Improve overall operational decision-making
🏢 QSR Industry Context

Quick Service Restaurants (QSRs) operate on:

Fast service & high customer turnover
Standardized menus
Scalable business models
Common Business Models:
Franchise model
Company-owned model
Hybrid model
Cloud kitchens
Aggregator-driven (Zomato/Swiggy)

🔍 Key Business Insights

1. 📈 Demand is time-dependent (peaks during lunch & dinner)

2. 🍟 Few menu items generate majority of revenue (Pareto effect)

3. 🚚 Delivery delays increase during peak hours

4. 👥 A small group of customers drives most revenue

5. 🔁 Customer retention is relatively low

6. 💳 Digital payments dominate (UPI/cards)

7. 🏪 Store performance varies significantly

8. 📦 Inventory is often mismanaged (overstock/understock)

💡 Business Recommendations

1. Optimize staffing during peak hours
2. Promote high-performing menu items
3. Improve delivery routing & capacity
4. Introduce loyalty & retention programs
5. Use demand forecasting for inventory
6. Upsell combos to increase order value
📌 Conclusion

This project highlights how a data-centric approach can transform QSR operations. By leveraging analytics, businesses can enhance efficiency, improve customer satisfaction, and achieve scalable growth.
